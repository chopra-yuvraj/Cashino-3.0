About The Event

Where strategy meets fortune

CASHINO 3.0 is not your average college fest game — it's an electrifying token-based tournament where every participant starts on equal footing with 150 tokens. Navigate through 13 unique games, each designed to test your wits, luck, and strategic thinking. From the calculated risk of Minefield to the high-stakes bluffs of Liar Dice, every game is a chance to multiply your fortune — or lose it all.

The goal is simple: finish with the maximum number of tokens. There are no shortcuts — just smart play, sharp decisions, and a bit of luck. Will you play it safe or go all in?

General Guidelines & Rules

Read carefully before stepping into the arena

Token System
  • All players will receive 150 tokens each to start with.
  • Price: 1 unit = 1 token.
  • Registration Fee: ₹200 (inclusive of GST).
Game Rules
  • In every game, the bold lines state the winning/losing criteria with their multiplier.
  • Once tokens used are non-refundable for each and every game.
  • Each game has its own set of specific rules and multipliers — check the individual game pages for details.
Fair Play & Disputes
  • If there is UFM (Unfair Means) in any game, the player loses his pledged tokens.
  • If there is any mistake from the house side, the player receives back his tokens.
  • Any dispute will be resolved by the event coordinator.
Judgement Criteria
  • The participant with the maximum amount of tokens remaining at the end of all games wins the tournament.
  • Strategy, risk management, and smart play are the keys to victory.
