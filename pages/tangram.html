Tangram

♦ 10 Tokens ♠ Manager: Anmol

♠ How to Play

  • Individual player game.
  • The player is given 7 Tangram pieces (tans).
  • A target pattern is shown - you must recreate it using all 7 pieces.
  • Time limit: 3 minutes.
  • All pieces must be used; no overlapping allowed.

♦ Outcomes

Result Multiplier
Solved within 3 minutes 2x
Not solved / Time expired 0x - Lose Bet
Tip: Start with the large triangles first to define the overall shape, then fit in the smaller pieces. Practice spatial reasoning!
