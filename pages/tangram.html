Tangram

🪙 10 Tokens 👤 Manager: Akshat

📖 How to Play

  • You will be given 7 tangram pieces.
  • The house gives you a random pattern to recreate.
  • You must use all 7 pieces to make the given pattern.
  • You have 30 seconds to solve the puzzle.
  • Pattern sheets with solutions are provided to the game manager.

💰 Winning / Losing

Outcome Multiplier
✅ Solved correctly within 30 seconds 1.5x
❌ Failed to solve in time 0x — Lose Tokens
💡 Tip: Start with the largest pieces first. The big triangle and parallelogram usually dictate the overall shape!
