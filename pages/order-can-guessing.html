Order Can Guessing

♦ 10 Tokens ♠ Manager: Hemanya

♠ How to Play

  • You are given 5 cans and 3 chances to arrange them in the correct order.
  • After each attempt, the game manager will tell you the number of correct and wrong cans.
  • Every time the manager states the correctness of the order, 1 chance is deducted.
  • Use the feedback to narrow down the correct arrangement!

♦ Outcomes & Multipliers

Result (after 3 chances) Multiplier
All 5 correct — Perfect Order! 2x
3 correct cans in order 0.5x
2 or fewer correct 0x — Lose All
Tip: Use the manager's feedback strategically. Each hint costs you a chance — make them count!
