♠ How to Play

  • There are 25 tiles on the board with mathematical symbols: %, $, +, {}, @, #, *
  • Tiles are displayed for 10 seconds - memorize the pattern!
  • After the display time, the pattern will be shown and you must flip tiles in the exact order of the pattern.
  • You get 2 chances to mess up. Every wrong tile resets your progress and you must start from the beginning.
  • You cannot jumble the order of the pattern - it must be exact.
  • Patterns are randomly assigned - there are 10 total patterns of varying difficulty.

♦ Winning Multipliers

Tip: You only have 10 seconds to memorize the pattern. Focus on the sequence, not just the symbols - order matters!
