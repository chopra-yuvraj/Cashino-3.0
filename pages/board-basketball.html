📖 How to Play

  • Each player gets 3 chances to score baskets placed on the board.
  • Your reward is based on how many baskets you score out of 3.
  • Aim carefully — each basket counts!

💰 Scoring & Multipliers

Baskets Scored Multiplier
🏀🏀🏀 3 Baskets — Perfect! 2x
🏀🏀 2 Baskets 1.5x
🏀 1 Basket 1x
❌ 0 Baskets 0x — Lose All
💡 Tip: Even 1 basket gets you your tokens back (1x). Go for accuracy over power!
