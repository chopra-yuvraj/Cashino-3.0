Board Basketball

♦ 10 Tokens ♠ Manager: Shreyas

♠ How to Play

  • Individual player game.
  • The player gets 3 chances to score baskets.
  • Each successful shot earns points toward a higher multiplier.
  • Score is based on total baskets made out of 3 attempts.

♦ Outcomes & Multipliers

Baskets Scored Multiplier
3 out of 3 3x
2 out of 3 2x
1 out of 3 1x (Tokens Returned)
0 out of 3 0x — Lose All
Tip: Aim with consistency. Scoring at least 1 basket guarantees you keep your tokens. Going for all 3 gives you a massive 3x return!
