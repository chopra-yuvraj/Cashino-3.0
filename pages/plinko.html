Plinko

♦ 4–6 Tokens ♠ Manager: Aditya

♠ How to Play

  • Drop the ball from the top of the Plinko board.
  • The ball bounces through pegs randomly and lands in a slot at the bottom.
  • The slot determines the number of tokens won or prize awarded.
  • Participants must purchase tokens from the official fest token counter.
  • No cash transactions permitted at the stall.
  • Price: 4 tokens per drop, or 6 tokens for 2 drops.
