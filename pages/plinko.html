Plinko

♦ 4–6 Tokens ♠ Manager: Manav

♠ How to Play

  • Drop the ball from the top of the Plinko board.
  • The ball bounces through pegs and lands in a slot at the bottom.
  • Each slot has a different multiplier value.
  • The Jackpot slots (center) are hardest to land in but give the highest reward.
  • Price is 4 tokens per ball; you may buy up to 6 tokens worth.

♦ Slot Multipliers

Slot Multiplier
Premium Slot 3x
Jackpot Slot 2x
Mid Slot 1.5x
Low Slot 1x
Edge Slot 0x - Lose Bet
Tip: The ball's path is random after each peg bounce. Premium slots are on the edges of the center - high risk, high reward!
