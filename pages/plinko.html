Plinko

🪙 4 Tokens (1 drop) · 6 Tokens (2 drops) 👤 Manager: Aditya

📖 How to Play

  • Drop a ball from the top of the vertical Plinko board.
  • The ball travels through pegs randomly and lands in a slot at the bottom.
  • The slot determines the number of tokens won or prize awarded.
  • Participants must purchase tokens from the official fest token counter.
  • No cash transactions permitted at the stall.
