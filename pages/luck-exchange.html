📖 How to Play

  • 3–5 players per game.
  • Each player is randomly allotted an envelope with a hidden multiplier.
  • Each player can open and check their own envelope.
  • Each player must make at least 1 exchange with another player.
  • Players can talk among themselves to negotiate exchanges.
  • Players cannot say or enact the multiplier amount to other players in any way.
  • The multiplier in your envelope at the end of exchanges determines your winnings.
