Colour Wheel of Fortune

♦ 10–40 Tokens (max) ♠ Manager: Saarthi

♠ How to Play

  • 1–4 players can play per round.
  • The wheel has 8 distinct colours across 16 parts.
  • Each player bets tokens on a colour of their choice.
  • The wheel is spun — only 1 colour wins.
  • Winnings are determined by the colour's multiplier.

♦ Colour Multipliers

Colour Slot 1 Slot 2
Light Yellow 0x 0x
Blue 1x 1x
Green 0x 0x
Black 1.5x 2x
White 0x 1x
Brown 0x 1x
Red 1.5x 1.5x
Dark Yellow 0x 1x
Tip: Black has the highest potential payout at 2x but only 2 out of 16 slots. Red offers consistent 1.5x on both slots!
