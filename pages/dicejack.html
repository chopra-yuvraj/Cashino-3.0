Dicejack

♦ 10–40 Tokens ♠ Manager: Kriish

♠ How to Play

  • Single player game against the dealer.
  • Player must bet at the start of the game.
  • Both the dealer and the player are provided with 2 dice each at the start of Round 1.
  • In Round 2, the player must roll another dice (compulsory).
  • After Round 2, the player is given a choice to roll or stay.
  • After the player stays, the dealer rolls. If the dealer's total is less than 17 after 3 rolls, the dealer rolls again. If the sum is ≥ 17, the dealer stays.
  • The dealer rolls dice visible to the player at all times.

♦ Winning / Losing

Outcome Multiplier
Player's total beats dealer's total 1.5x
Player loses to dealer 0x - Lose Bet
Tip: Think of it like Blackjack but with dice! Know when to roll and when to stay. The dealer must hit until 17 - use this to your advantage.
