Snake Pit

🪙 10 Tokens 👤 Manager: Hardik

📖 How to Play

  • You get 3 balls to attempt the course.
  • There are 3 stages throughout the course — each progressively harder.
  • Players can attempt to fulfil any stage.
  • On completion of any stage, the player cannot play further even if chances are remaining — they must cash out at the scored multiplier.
  • If the ball falls off the board, 1 chance is deducted until 0 are left.
  • Stages will be marked on the board.

💰 Stage Multipliers

Stage Multiplier on Completion
🟢 Stage 1 1.2x
🟡 Stage 2 1.5x
🔴 Stage 3 2x
❌ All 3 balls lost 0x — Lose All
💡 Tip: Once you complete a stage, you're locked in at that multiplier. Decide if you want the safe 1.2x or risk going for the 2x!
