7 Up & Down

🪙 Min 1 — Max 60 Tokens per round 👤 Manager: Abhijay

📖 How to Play

  • Player places tokens on ONE of three sections: 7 Down, Lucky 7, or 7 Up.
  • Once betting is closed, the volunteer rolls both dice in clear view.
  • The total is announced and tokens are distributed based on the result.
  • Dice must be shown before the first round.
  • Rolls must be visible to all players.
  • No physical interference allowed.
  • Any dispute will be resolved by the event coordinator.

💰 Winning Multipliers

Bet Section Condition Multiplier
⬇️ 7 Down Dice total is below 7 1.5x
⭐ Lucky 7 Dice total is exactly 7 3x
⬆️ 7 Up Dice total is above 7 1.5x
❌ Wrong Bet Your section didn't match 0x — Lose Bet
💡 Tip: Lucky 7 pays 3x but is the hardest to hit. 7 Up and 7 Down are safer bets at 1.5x!
