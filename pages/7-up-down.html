7 Up & Down

♦ 1–60 Tokens ♠ Manager: Abhijay

♠ How to Play

  • Player places tokens on one of the three sections before the roll.
  • Two dice are thrown. The sum of dice determines the outcome.
  • Players bet on one of three options before the roll:
  • 7 Down (Ander Bahar): Sum is 2–6
  • Lucky 7: Sum is exactly 7
  • 7 Up (Upar Bahar): Sum is 8–12

♦ Outcomes & Multipliers

Bet Option Condition Multiplier
7 Down Sum is 2–6 1.5x
Lucky 7 Sum is exactly 7 3x
7 Up Sum is 8–12 1.5x
Wrong Bet - 0x - Lose Bet
Tip: Lucky 7 has the highest payout at 3x but the lowest probability. 7 Down and 7 Up are safer bets with 1.5x returns. Max individual bet is 60 tokens.
