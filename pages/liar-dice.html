Liar Dice

♦ 6 Tokens (initial) · Raises in multiples of 6 ♠ Manager: Vasu

♠ How to Play

  • 2–3 players per game.
  • There are the same number of rounds as the number of players.
  • There can be a max of 5 raises/challenges in a single round.
  • At the start of a round, a player states a condition relating to the count of a number on the dice and bets tokens.
  • The next player in order must challenge the bet or raise the bet.
  • If a player raises, the next player must then challenge or raise - this cycle continues until someone challenges or max raises are reached.
  • After a challenge, the dice are revealed and the condition is verified.

♦ Winning / Losing Criteria

Outcome Result
Condition is TRUE Person who stated the condition wins the pot
Condition is FALSE Person who challenged wins the pot
Key Terms:
Raise: The player states a new condition (overrides previous) and bets token-5.
Challenge: The player ends the round, betting that the stated condition is NOT true. Dice are revealed after a challenge or max raise limit.
