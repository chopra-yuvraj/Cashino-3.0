Minefield

♦ 10 Tokens ♠ Manager: Aarav

♠ How to Play

  • Individual player game.
  • The player has to flip the tiles to reveal a mine or a gem.
  • The arrangement of the tiles should not be seen by the player.
  • Total 25 tiles on the board.
  • The player selects a difficulty level (number of mines) before starting.
  • If a mine is revealed, the game ends and all tokens are lost.
  • The player can choose to cash out at any gem milestone to keep their winnings.
