Luck Ladder

🪙 10 Tokens 👤 Manager: Nimisha

📖 How to Play

  • The player rolls the dice once to determine the colour category.
  • There are four different coloured chits: Red, Blue, Green, and White.
  • The player must pick one chit from the colour determined by the dice roll.
  • The chit will contain an instruction or task linked to a specific game.
  • The player must redeem or perform the task in the respective game as mentioned on the chit.
  • Failure to redeem or complete the instruction will result in immediate disqualification.

🎨 Colour Categories

Colour Description
Red High-risk task from a random game
Blue Medium-risk task from a random game
Green Low-risk task from a random game
White Wildcard — anything goes!
⚠️ Important: Failure to complete your chit task results in immediate disqualification from the tournament. This game is a gamble on what destiny hands you!
