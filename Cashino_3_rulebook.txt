Liar Dice
Liar Dice:


Price: initial amount(to play the round ): 6 tokens
        Raises,betting in multiple of 6
Manager: VASU
Rules:
1. 2-3 players
2. There are same number of rounds as the number of players
3. There can be max 5 raises/ challenges in a single round
4. At the start of the round a players states a condition relating to the count of the number on the dice and bets tokens
5. The 2nd person in order has to challenge the bet* or raise the bet*.
6. If the 2nd person raises the bet , the 3rd player has to challenge the bet or raise the bet and the cycle will continue until someone challenges the raised bet or max challenges /raises are reached.
7. After challenging the condition, the dices are revealed and the condition stated is verified
8. If the condition is true: the person who stated the condition wins the pot
9. If the condition is false: the person who challenged the condition wins the pot


Raises bet* - the player who raises has to state a new condition that overrides the previous condition if there is any. And has to bet a token-5
Challenging* - the player who challenges the condition ends the rounds and bets on the fact if the condition is not true. The remaining player’s opinion is then not considered.the dices are revealed after some player challenges the condition or they hit the max limit of condition/raises.
